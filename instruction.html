INSTRUCTIONS

SETUP
one player thinks of a word and draws dashes for each letter. The other player guesses letters to uncover the word.
GUESSING
Players take turns guessing letters if a guessed letters in the word.It's guessed letter in the word, It's filled into its correct positions.If not a body part of the hangmnan is drawn.
WINING OR LOSING
The game ends when the word is guessed correctly,or the hangman is completed.If the word is guessed , the guessing players wins.Otherwise,the players who thought of the word wins.
VARIATIONS
EASY: Use short ,common words.

MEDIUM: Use longer words or phrases.

DIFFCULT: Use longest common or obscure words.

STRATEGY Keep track of guessed letters to avoid repeating them, and strategically guess based on common letter patterns in words.
